Frequently Asked Questions

A wild guess on what the questions will be

  • Who can you get to connect with the regular users?

    Our target for the "helper" position are organizations or entities willing to give a hand to those who need it. At first instance we thought this could be a humanitarian way for students to get volunteering oportunities.

  • How would you classify people in the system?

    Having each user to fill a form, that will be given to a model and then they can be categorized on how fast the require the connection.

  • What problem does this solve?

    This projects looks to solve the lack of human interaction for those people primarily in isolation conditions by these individuals with people looking to help.

  • Why do you like this idea?

    The main reason we fell in love with this idea was because it tackles the lack of human interaction that someone in isolation can be going through. Added to the fact that this idea has so many scalability options, at some point in the brainstorming procress we even considered adding a tab for local businesses recommendations.
